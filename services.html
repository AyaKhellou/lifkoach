Frequently Asked Questions

How long is each coaching session?

Each coaching session is 60 minutes long. This gives us enough time to dive deep into your goals, challenges, and action plans while maintaining focus and energy throughout the session.

Can I switch between coaching programs?

Yes, you can switch between programs at any time. We understand that your needs may change, and we're flexible to adjust your coaching plan accordingly.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours notice will be counted as completed sessions.

Do you offer virtual coaching sessions?

Yes! All our coaching sessions are available both in-person and virtually via video call, giving you the flexibility to choose what works best for you.

How quickly will I see results?

Results vary by individual and depend on your goals, commitment level, and circumstances. Most clients report noticeable improvements within 4-6 weeks of consistent coaching.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee for your first session. If you're not completely satisfied, we'll provide a full refund. For packages, refunds are prorated based on unused sessions.
